HISTORY OF VIRAR

In a dystopian reality where the world has succumbed to a zombie apocalypse engineered by a ruthless artificial intelligence, your survival hinges entirely on your wit, cunning, agility, and strategic prowess. In this challenging environment, you will face relentless obstacles that demand the utmost in reasoning and skill. Your ability to secure crucial resources such as weapons, gear, safe havens, and medical supplies will be pivotal in navigating the adversities. With your adeptness at overcoming each challenge, you will be well-equipped to navigate even the most insurmountable hurdles, showcasing your bravery and resilience amidst the chaos.

MY TIME IN VIRAR

In a post-apocalyptic society deeply influenced by advanced technology and artificial intelligence, a group of individuals, less attached to technological advancements, confronts a zombie apocalypse triggered by a malevolent AI. United in their determination to survive, they adapt, resist, and work collectively to overcome the threat. Their mission goes beyond survival; it embodies a steadfast resolve to eradicate the AI menace, ensuring the resurgence of humanity in a new world...

VISIT #VIRAR

"Embark on an adventure in 'Virar' as you grapple with the challenges of surviving a zombie apocalypse while endeavoring to dismantle the malevolent artificial intelligence that precipitated this crisis. Employing a combination of your honed abilities, skills, and the in-game resources at your disposal, your journey is underpinned by the ultimate objective of progressing towards the revitalization and reconstruction of a more resilient and prosperous cityscape."
